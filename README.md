## Overview

This project analyzes Uber trip data across 5 major Indian cities to uncover operational patterns, revenue distribution, and efficiency gaps. Built as an interactive Power BI dashboard with driver-level, city-level, and time-based filters.

Key finding: Chennai leads in total revenue despite Delhi having higher trip volume, and UPI is the dominant payment method at 40% of all transactions.

---

## Dashboard Highlights

- **90.53%** completed trip rate | **9.47%** cancellation rate
- **4.29** average driver rating | **12.66 km** average trip distance
- **Total Trips by City** — Chennai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Bengaluru comparison
- **Total Revenue by City** — Chennai and Bengaluru lead in revenue/km
- **Payment Type Breakdown** — UPI (40%), Cash (32.63%), Card (27.37%)
- **Trip Status** — 86 completed vs 9 cancelled trips
- **Correlation between Distance and Fare** — scatter plot analysis
- **Revenue/km by Month and Day** — time-series trend analysis
- **Rating by Month and Day** — driver performance over time
- **Sum of Revenue by Driver** — individual driver performance ranking
